Sailor Moon

Kaitlin's Magical Sailor Moon Page!

“I am Sailor Moon, the champion of justice. In the name of the moon, I will right wrong and triumph over evil… and that means you!”

Sailor moon is instantly recognizable to anyone with a memory of the 90's. While there are many great anime heroes to choose from, I selected the the scout of justice. Sailor moon has been an important character in my life not only due to nostalgia but also inspiration. She was a positive influence for young girls to see that it is okay to be strong for yourself and your friends, while also still showing your emotions! Sailor Moon is a great example of the importance of mind over matter (positivity) as well as standing up for what you believe in.

Here are some fun facts about Sailor Moon!
She.....
  • Loves food, especially ice cream!
  • Dislikes math.
  • Is a romantic at heart.
  • Can kick some evil butt when necessary!
